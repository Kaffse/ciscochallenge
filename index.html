What's this about?

Cisco have kindly donated 18 Smartcitizen kits to the University of Glasgow; these outstanding little devices can record all kinds of interesting data: light, sound, CO2, NO... With these awesome devices the University of Glasgow is running a challenge over the month of June 2014, sponsored by Cisco, allowing you to show the world just what the future could look like. With the opening event on the 28th May 2014 as a keynote and chance for teams to form and ideas to flow, the challenge will begin. Each team is given a month to formulate, then show off their idea. A winner will be chosen and presented with Cisco hardware, as well as their entry showcased at the Glasgow Science Centre at the Cisco stand.
